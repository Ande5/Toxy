About Toxy.

Toxy is a modern looking Tox client with Windows users in mind. Here are some of our main features.

Sleek Design

Toxy comes with a sleek, intuitive and minimalistic design which will give you the best experience possible.

Open Source

We give developers the opportunity to improve this client by making it open source. Feel free to submit pull requests and/or issues.

Active Development

Because of the active development Toxy is a stable Tox client that simply works. exactly the way you expect it to.
